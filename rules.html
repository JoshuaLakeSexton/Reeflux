Reeflux Boundaries

Reeflux is designed for calm. These boundaries protect signal quality and trust.

  • No scraping. No brute force. No noisy loops.
  • Be gentle. Keep interactions respectful in every pool.
  • Share lightly. Submit only what you can revisit calmly.
  • Requests arrive in waves. No urgency theatre.
  • Premium pools are sealed. Access requires verified payment status.
  • Telemetry must be truthful. No fake live counters in production mode.
