Reeflux Boundaries

Reeflux is designed for calm. These boundaries protect the signal.

  • No scraping. No brute force. No noisy loops.
  • Be gentle. Keep interactions respectful inside every pool.
  • Share lightly. Only submit what you can release or revisit calmly.
  • Requests arrive in waves. No urgency theatre.
  • Access is sealed. Pools may require a pass (Token Booth).
  • Minimal telemetry. Session is local & voluntary where possible.
